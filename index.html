Chapter 01

Structure before spectacle.

I’m Yash Choudhary—a designer-engineer from Jaipur, pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science at ACEIT. With four years of UI/UX experience and a year deep in the Salesforce ecosystem, I operate where visual craft meets platform engineering.

My design foundation—years spent mastering Figma, motion design, and complex visual systems—informs every architecture I build. This expertise spans pixel-perfect React interfaces, immersive GSAP-driven animations, scalable Lightning components, and next-gen Agentforce AI integrations. As an elite Trailhead Ranger with 150+ badges, I continuously push the boundaries of CRM development, UI architecture, performance tuning, and full-stack system integrations. Currently interning at Infotact Solutions and Fleeca, I have shipped production-grade interfaces for real clients while scaling my own Salesforce Architecture Lab.

Beyond the screen, I am a constant explorer of vast genres of novels (from sci-fi to philosophy), an avid follower of seasonal anime, and a competitive gamer. These diverse creative outlets feed directly back into my technical engineering work: narrative storytelling from deep novels, dynamic visual rhythm from anime, and strategic systems thinking from high-level gaming all fundamentally shape how I architect clean, intuitive, and unforgettable digital experiences.
